Welcome to

HiAST

Hyderabad Institute of Arts, Science And Technology (Sindhi: حيدرآباد انسٽيٽيوٽ آف آرٽس، سائنس اينڊ ٽيڪنالاجي) or HiAST is a private degree awarding institution located in Hyderabad, Pakistan. It was established on 1 August 2001, and in February 2013 was chartered under The Hyderabad Institute of Arts, Science and Technology Act, 2013.[1] It has Higher Education Commission of Pakistan-recognized affiliations with both the University of Sindh and Mehran University of Engineering and Technology.[2][3][4]
